The Effect of in-school and out-school life on student score and grade and their charactristics.¶

by Marawan Abdelrahman¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this Invistagtion, I wanted to know the effect of student's life in school and outside school and determine their effect on student's score and grade compared to other students in thesame country.

Dataset Overview¶

This Dataset contains a survey of students' skills at the end of compulasory(obligatory) education in 68 different location around the world and describes how well are the student beyond this education. This aslo contains the scores of 485490 student who conducted PISA 2012 assesment in science, math and reading.

Distribution of the time consumed by students to learn math and science¶

In this Visualization we can see that that the range of time students consume to learn math and science ranges between 100 and 500 minutes per week (1:40 to 8:20 hours) and there is concentration around 200 minutes (3:20 hour). We can see that students take less time to learn math than science.
